Erada means

"Those on the Outside"

Erada Collective seeks to improve healthcare outcomes as the use of technology increases, including for marginalised/underrepresented groups who are so often left on the "outside".

Within an inter-disciplinary community of practice (CoP), we support professionals and innovators to enhance their own professional fulfilment through critically evaluated, co-designed data and inclusive digital practices and innovations.

What We Do

Erada Collective enables collaborations to operationalise responsible technology principles into real-world innovation for better health and social outcomes.

Innovation where healthcare professional's challenges and real-world experiences inform equitable, inclusive, and user-friendly technology development implementation, and governance.

The core values that guide our work are: Equity & Inclusion in outcome through collaboration while ensuring the wellbeing of professionals to achieve Innovation, and Efficiency in systems.

We do this work through:

  • Interdisciplinary Research
  • Events
  • Collaborative Innovation
  • Advisory and Governance Services
